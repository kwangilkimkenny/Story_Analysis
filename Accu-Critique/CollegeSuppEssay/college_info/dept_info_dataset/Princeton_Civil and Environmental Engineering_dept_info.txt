School Name: 
Department: Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering
Major: Civil and Environmental Engineering (B.S.E. / M.Eng. / M.S.E. / Ph.D.)


The department offers undergraduates five options: architecture and engineering, environmental engineering, geological engineering, structural engineering, and engineering and the liberal arts. The graduate program offers study and research in two areas: mechanics, materials, and structures, and environmental engineering and water resources.
